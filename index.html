What exactly is a Hackathon?

Don't worry, we are not actually hacking in the sense of the word "hacking!"

A hackathon is a place for creators to form a team and work on a project relating to computer science and other fields of STEAM. These projects will be shared with everyone and we will all have an opportunity to appreciate the hard work and creativity put into these amazing projects.

Henry the hippo needs your help! He was last seen sobbing as he trudged away from his former habitat (destroyed by humans ofc).

After talking to Chris the crocodile, he has come to the revelation that he needs you (yes you!) to help him accomplish his aspirations to better him and his species!

The What

Hippo Hack is a 12 hour hackathon held in July where programmers of all experience levels across the Greater Boston area will congregate for a day full of collaboration, teamwork and fun!

The How

Teams will choose a theme from a pre- determined list of themes/topics, and by the end of the hacking time, the judging phase will begin and winners will be determined. Prizes will not only be awarded for the best overall projects, but also to beginners that display remarkable progress.

FAQ

I have ZERO programming experience. Should I come?

OF COURSE! In fact, judging criteria will consider experience for those who don't have a leg up. We will run a number of introductory classes during the day if you are just starting out!

How much does it cost?

It's completely free! Participants will receive complimentary merchandise, food, and more!

How many people can be in a group?

3-5 people can be in a group. You can also work in smaller groups or even solo. If you need a group, we can help you find one to work in!

What should I bring?

Bring your computer and a charger as well as anything else, including other tech-related gear you may want, so that you can work as productively as you can. However, we will be bringing spare gear and we will be able to loan out Chromebooks if you don't own a computer or you forget to bring yours :)

What age do I have to be in order to participate?

Hippo Hack is for all grades 6-12!
